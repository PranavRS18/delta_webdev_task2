THE COLLAPSE OF CYBERIA:


The collapsing megacity of Cyberia stands on the edge of total ruin. Once powered by the near-omniscient central AI system known as AUREX(Autonomous Unified Resource Extraction eXecutive), Cyberia thrived in perfect machine-governed harmony. But now, AUREX is faltering. Core systems are failing, and rogue bots are turning against the few surviving humans.

Scattered across the endless remains of Cyberia, vital data keys, shard vaults, bot factories, and old military constructs still function — some erratically. As one of the last operatives sent by the Resistance, your mission is to stabilize outposts, harvest resources, and stay alive.

But beware — AUREX still watches from behind the veil, and its fractured mind may be both your enemy and your only chance at survival.



CONTROLS:


Use the following keys to move:
W – Move Up
A – Move Left
S – Move Down
D – Move Right



OBJECTIVES:


- Collect keys scattered across the terrain.
- Deposit them into Central Hubs to convert keys into shards.
- AUREX's Health is depleting over time, deliver shards to Base Stations to recover AUREX's health.
- Repeat and survive. The longer you last, the more you restore order — and uncover Cyberia’s secrets.



SAFE ZONES:


Safe Zones are your only sanctuary. Inside:
- You are immune to all damage.
- You can rest and plan.
- Press 'M' to access the Marketplace.



MARKETPLACE:


Press 'M' while in Safe Zones to spend shards on power-ups. These include:

Ultimate Tracker
Reveals locations of Central Hubs and Base Stations for 60 seconds.

Large Health Pack
Restores 20 Health instantly to the Player.

Key Magnet
Automatically attracts nearby keys to you.

EMP Blast
Emits a short-range pulse that disables nearby shooters and bots temporarily.

Increase Ammo Capacity
Lets you store and fire more shots before needing to reload.

Upgrade Firearm
Enhances your weapon’s damage, rate of fire.

Data Mine
A passive resource generator that slowly produces shards over time.

Teleportation Pods
Place two points to fast-travel between them instantly.



WORLD FEATURES:


- The world is infinite and ever-expanding.
- New threats, structures, and secrets are dynamically generated.
- Learn your surroundings, observe patterns, and adapt quickly.



ENEMIES & HAZARDS:


- Buildings: Static obstacles that block movement.
- Rotating Shooters: Deadly sentries that rotate and deal damage on contact.
- Bot Factories: Dangerous structures that constantly spawn bots. The rate increases over time.
Avoid them unless you’re ready to fight.



POWER-UPS IN THE WORLD:


- Health Packs: Instantly restore 10 HP.
- Invisibility Packs: Grant 10 seconds of invisibility, allowing you to pass bots and shooters undetected.



STATS:


Keep an eye on your vital stats at all times:
- Top Left:
  • System Health
  • Key Count
  • Shard Count

- Top Right:
  • Player Health
  • Score

- Bottom Right:
  • Player Position — Current Grid coordinates.



SURVIVAL TIPS:


- Deposit keys regularly to reduce risk.
- Use Safe Zones wisely — rest, plan, and upgrade.
- Avoid tight corners near rotating shooters.
- Time your invisibility to escape or infiltrate.
- Spend shards intelligently; one power-up can change everything.
- Stay moving. Standing still invites death.



SUMMARY:


You are the last thread in the unraveling fabric of Cyberia. Collect. Upgrade. Survive. Resist the broken machines, master the systems, and uncover the fate of AUREX — and maybe, rewrite the future.
